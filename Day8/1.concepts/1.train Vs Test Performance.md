# Training Performance vs Testing Performance

## Training Performance

Training performance measures how well a model performs on the **training data** used to learn patterns.

## Testing Performance

Testing performance measures how well the trained model performs on **unseen (test) data**.

## Difference

| Training Performance | Testing Performance |
|----------------------|---------------------|
| Evaluated on training data. | Evaluated on unseen test data. |
| Measures how well the model learns. | Measures how well the model generalizes to new data. |

> A good model should have **high and similar** training and testing performance.
